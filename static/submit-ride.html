Submit Your Own Ride

Help map Richmond's bike lane hazards by contributing a recording.

What You Need

A video file Camera mounted to your bike, pointed forward. MP4 or MOV.
A GPX file GPS track from a phone app or bike computer, exported as .gpx.
Start your camera and GPS tracker at the same time. The system uses timestamps to match each video frame to a location — if they're out of sync, hazards will appear at the wrong spot on the map.

Recording Your GPX Track

Any app that records a GPS route and exports a .gpx file will work:

  • GPX Tracker recommended
    Simple and free. Start a track, ride, stop and export as GPX.
  • Strava gpx export untested
    Record as normal, then export GPX from the activity page on the website (not the app).
  • Ride with GPS gpx export untested
    Record a ride, then export GPX from the activity page on the website.
